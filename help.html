Welcome to the Ocean Restoration Help Page

This page will guide you through the different sections of our website and help you find the information you need.

Navigation Menu

The navigation menu is located at the top of every page. It includes the following links:

  • Home: Takes you to the homepage where you can find an overview of our mission and latest news.
  • Learn: Provides educational resources about ocean conservation and restoration efforts.
  • About: Gives detailed information about our organization and team.
  • Help: Brings you to this help page for assistance with navigating the website.

Enabling Notifications

To receive notifications about upcoming events and important updates, click the "Enable Notifications" button on the homepage.

Countdown Timer

To see a countdown timer for Ocean Cleaning Day, click the "Enable Countdown" button on the homepage. The timer will display the remaining time until the event.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions and answers that might help you:

  • How do I join an event? Visit the Events page to see upcoming events and register.
  • Can I volunteer remotely? Yes, we offer remote volunteering opportunities. Check the Volunteer page for more details.
  • Where can I find more educational resources? Visit the Resources page for articles, videos, and more.
